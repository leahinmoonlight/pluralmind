# Adding Pluralmind to your OBS Chat Dock

You can enable Pluralmind support in your OBS Chat Dock with just a few clicks! This will let you see plural messages directly in your OBS chat.

::: tip Prefer to watch how it's done?
We also have a [video tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G11KOhQJOoE) that goes through all the steps.
:::

## Enabling Pluralmind

1. On the left side of your OBS Settings (File -> Settings), choose the **Stream** tab.
2. Under **Advanced Options**, set **Twitch Chat Add-Ons** to **FrankerFaceZ**. (You can also choose **BetterTTV and FrankerFaceZ**.)
3. Click **OK** to save your settings. Your OBS docks may refresh.
4. Open FFZ's settings by clicking on the gear (**Chat Settings**) in your OBS Chat Dock, then **Switch to Non-Mod Settings** and choose **FrankerFaceZ Control Center**.
5. On the left side, choose **Add-Ons**.
6. Search for "Pluralmind" in the search bar at the top, and **Enable** it.
7. Close FFZ's settings, then right-click on your chat dock and choose **Refresh**.

That's it! Feel free to reach out on our [Discord](https://discord.gg/3TseAS2fne) if you have any questions.
